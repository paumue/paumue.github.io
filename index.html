Hi there! This is my wobsite.

My name is Paul. I code, build products and sometimes consult. I am an avid reader but definitely not a frontend dev.

Most of the time, I read, learn and build stuff to solve two challenges I deeply care about:

  • Education isn't working anymore. Currently education focuses more on the institutions than on the individuals themselves. The technology we built is not helping us to learn better, but rather it is addictive and hinders learning. Learners are put into boxes and made to learn in specific frameworks. We need to build an affordable, technology-powered education system with the learners’s needs at the core.
  • The internet is broken. We don't have data ownership and people are not incentivised to create great content since only big tech profits. We need to build a privacy-first internet where users have absolute data ownership and content creators can monetise their work.

If you feel passionate about theses challenges too, get in touch!
