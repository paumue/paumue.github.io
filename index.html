About

I am currently studying Computer Science with Intelligent Systems at King's College London. Before that, I was doing the European Voluntary Service in Siauliai, Lithuania. For a year, I worked at an international school and taught German classes. Moreover, I organised workshops about different topics. For instance, we organised a science youth camp and youth exchanges about the future of Europe.

I started programming when I was around 15 years old. However, before university I never had formal computer science education and I was completely self-taught.

For my final thesis in high school, I programmed a Lego Mindstorms EV3 robot in Java, as well as, in the graphical programming language provided by Lego. The robot is able to autonomously clean a table. To achieve this, I used the Subsumption architecture. You can find the code here and the abstract of the thesis here.
